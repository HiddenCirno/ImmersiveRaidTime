# Project Spark · Immersive Raid Time

Sync your in-game time with reality.

**What could it do?** 

- Syncs your raid time completely with real-world time, with options to freely adjust your timezone and toggle Daylight Saving Time (DST).

- Enables "Immersive Mode," which replaces the top-right raid countdown timer with a real-world clock. The raid duration becomes infinite, and the MIA (Missing In Action) mechanic is completely disabled.

- Provides secondary QOL (Quality of Life) improvements: single-pressing the 'O' key will now also display the real-world time on the HUD indicator (this specific real-time display is unaffected by custom timezone settings).

**How to install?** 

- ownload the latest release from the Releases section on the right. Extract the archive and simply drop the `BepInEx` folder into your game's root directory, overwriting if necessary.

**Compatibility?**

- **Fika:** Fully compatible. I haven't encountered any issues during my actual testing.
- **Fika.Co-op:** Unsure/Untested. 
- **Fika.Headless:** Unsure/Untested. Same as above.
